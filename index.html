About the Event

MAIN Segment 2025 is Egypt's premier mechanical engineering career experience, designed to connect passionate students with the future of industry, automation, and sustainable technology.

Mechanical-Focused Workshops

Explore nine specialized workshops designed to enhance your mechanical engineering skills

Six Sigma / Lean Production

+

An introduction to methodologies that boost manufacturing and service efficiency by eliminating waste, reducing variation, and improving quality. Covers Lean principles and the Six Sigma DMAIC process.

CFD with ANSYS Fluent

+

A practical overview of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) using ANSYS Fluent. Learn how engineers simulate fluid flow and heat transfer, and how to interpret simulation results in engineering analysis.

BIM for Mechanical Engineers

+

An introduction to Building Information Modeling (BIM) for mechanical engineers. Covers 3D design workflows, BIM tools, and how mechanical components are integrated into modern building projects.

Supply Chain Basics

+

A foundational session on logistics, procurement, inventory control, and how engineers contribute to building resilient and efficient supply chains across industries.

Fusion 360 for 3D Printing

+

Learn the basics of Autodesk Fusion 360 for modeling and preparing designs for 3D printing. Includes parametric design, best practices, and exporting print-ready models.

AI in Engineering

+

Explore how artificial intelligence is revolutionizing engineering with topics like AI-driven design, simulation, generative design, and smart systems in manufacturing.

Renewable Energy

+

An overview of renewable energy technologies, including solar, wind, and thermal energy systems. Covers how mechanical engineers design, analyze, and implement sustainable energy solutions.

Simulations with MATLAB

+

Learn how MATLAB and Simulink are used to simulate mechanical systems, analyze data, and run controlled simulations in academic and industrial applications.

Automotive, Robotics, and R&D

+

A combined session offering insights into automotive innovation, robotics fundamentals, and the role of R&D in driving engineering breakthroughs, from prototyping to real-world deployment.
